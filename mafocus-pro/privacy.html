Privacy Policy for MaFocus Pro

Last updated: 2026/04/28

This Privacy Policy describes how BluNivalis Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the MaFocus Pro Android app.

Information You Provide

MaFocus Pro is designed to let users create and manage focus timer content. Information you enter into the app, such as preset names, timer settings, focus labels, statistics, or similar content, is stored locally on your device.

Personal Data

We do not directly collect personal data from you through MaFocus Pro. We do not require account creation, and we do not provide cloud accounts or user profiles within the app at this time.

Local Storage

Data you create in MaFocus Pro is stored locally on your device. We do not intentionally transmit your focus timer content to our own servers.

Children's Privacy

MaFocus Pro is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own systems.

Data Security

We aim to keep the app and its dependencies reasonably up to date. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop all collection of information by the app by uninstalling it. You can also clear locally stored app data through your device settings, depending on your device and Android version.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: blunivalis@gmail.com
