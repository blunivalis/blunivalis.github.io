MaFace Pro

MaFace Pro is the premium version of MaFace for Android. It is designed for users who want an ad-free fullscreen front-camera mirror.

Features

  • Use a fullscreen front-camera mirror preview
  • Pinch to zoom for a closer look
  • Enable light mode with a bright screen frame
  • Capture photos saved locally to DCIM/MaFace
  • Premium version with no ads
