Privacy Policy for MaText

Last updated: 2026/04/29

This Privacy Policy describes how BluNivalis Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the MaText Android app.

Information You Provide

MaText is designed to let users type or paste text and apply local text transformations. Text you enter into the app, including input text and transformed output, is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Personal Data

We do not directly collect personal data from you through MaText. We do not require account creation, and we do not provide cloud accounts or user profiles within the app at this time.

Advertising

MaText may display ads through third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob. These third-party services may process certain device-related or advertising-related information in order to provide, improve, measure, or personalize ads, subject to their own terms and privacy practices.

Where required, MaText may show an advertising consent flow that allows you to manage choices related to advertising and related third-party processing.

MaText does not intentionally pass your input text or transformed output text to advertising services.

Local Storage

MaText may store app settings, preferences, theme choices, language choices, or similar app configuration locally on your device. MaText is not designed to permanently store your input text or transformed output text. We do not intentionally transmit your text content to our own servers.

Clipboard

MaText may read from the clipboard when you choose to paste text. MaText may write to the clipboard when you choose to copy output text. MaText does not monitor the clipboard in the background.

Third-Party Services

MaText may use third-party services that are necessary for app functionality or monetization. These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Example third-party service:

  • Google AdMob

Children's Privacy

MaText is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own systems.

Data Security

We aim to keep the app and its dependencies reasonably up to date. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop all collection of information by the app by uninstalling it. You can also clear locally stored app data through your device settings, depending on your device and Android version. Advertising-related choices may also be available through the app, your device settings, or third-party consent controls where applicable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: blunivalis@gmail.com
