Privacy Policy for MaText Pro

Last updated: 2026/04/29

This Privacy Policy describes how BluNivalis Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the MaText Pro Android app.

Information You Provide

MaText Pro is designed to let users type or paste text and apply local text transformations. Text you enter into the app, including input text and transformed output, is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Personal Data

We do not directly collect personal data from you through MaText Pro. We do not require account creation, and we do not provide cloud accounts or user profiles within the app at this time.

Advertising

MaText Pro does not display ads.

Local Storage

MaText Pro may store app settings, preferences, theme choices, language choices, or similar app configuration locally on your device. MaText Pro is not designed to permanently store your input text or transformed output text. We do not intentionally transmit your text content to our own servers.

Clipboard

MaText Pro may read from the clipboard when you choose to paste text. MaText Pro may write to the clipboard when you choose to copy output text. MaText Pro does not monitor the clipboard in the background.

Children's Privacy

MaText Pro is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own systems.

Data Security

We aim to keep the app and its dependencies reasonably up to date. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop all collection of information by the app by uninstalling it. You can also clear locally stored app data through your device settings, depending on your device and Android version.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: blunivalis@gmail.com
