Privacy Policy for MaCheck

Last updated: 2026/04/22

This Privacy Policy describes how BluNivalis Studio ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the MaCheck Android app.

Information You Provide

MaCheck is designed to let users create and manage checklist content. Information you enter into the app, such as checklist names, entries, or similar content, is stored locally on your device.

Personal Data

We do not directly collect personal data from you through MaCheck. We do not require account creation, and we do not provide cloud accounts or user profiles within the app at this time.

Advertising

MaCheck may display ads through third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob. These third-party services may process certain device-related or advertising-related information in order to provide, improve, measure, or personalize ads, subject to their own terms and privacy practices.

Local Storage

Data you create in MaCheck is stored locally on your device. We do not intentionally transmit your checklist content to our own servers.

Third-Party Services

MaCheck may use third-party services that are necessary for app functionality or monetization. These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Example third-party service:

  • Google AdMob

Children's Privacy

MaCheck is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own systems.

Data Security

We aim to keep the app and its dependencies reasonably up to date. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop all collection of information by the app by uninstalling it. You can also clear locally stored app data through your device settings, depending on your device and Android version.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
